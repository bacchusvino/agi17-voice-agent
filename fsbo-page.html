The Big Misunderstanding About Commission

New national rules mean:

Sellers don't have to offer buyer-agent commission

But buyer agents can still demand it in the offer

One signature in the wrong place → you end up paying it anyway

No written commission agreement = no commission owed

But many FSBO sellers agree to pay one without realizing it.
