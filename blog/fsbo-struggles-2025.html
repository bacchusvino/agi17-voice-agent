Where FSBO Sellers Are Getting Stuck

Paperwork Overload

California requires extensive disclosures: property condition reports, earthquake zone disclosures, fire zone disclaimers, HOA documentation, lead-based paint disclosures (if pre-1978), and more. One missing form can void the sale or trigger lawsuits months later.

Pricing Mistakes

Without a professional market analysis, FSBO sellers often overprice (scaring away buyers) or underprice (leaving money on the table). San Diego's diverse neighborhoods each have unique value drivers that require local expertise.

No Buyer Agent Network

Professional agents have established relationships with buyer agents across San Diego County. A FSBO seller has none. This means fewer qualified showings and less competitive bidding.

Negotiation Leverage Loss

Professional agents manage multiple offers, counteroffer strategy, and inspection negotiations. FSBO sellers often cave under pressure or miss better terms.
